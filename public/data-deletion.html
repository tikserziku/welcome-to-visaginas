Data Deletion Request - Welcome to Visaginas

We respect your right to control your data. You can request the deletion of your personal information from our application at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to [Your Contact Email]
  2. Use the subject line "Data Deletion Request - Welcome to Visaginas"
  3. In the body of the email, please include:
    • Your full name
    • The email address associated with your account
    • A clear statement requesting the deletion of your data

What Happens Next

Upon receiving your request, we will:

  1. Verify your identity to ensure the security of your data
  2. Process your request within 30 days
  3. Send you a confirmation email once your data has been deleted

Data We Will Delete

Upon your request, we will delete all personal information associated with your account, including:

  • Your account information
  • Any images you have uploaded
  • Generated greeting cards
  • Usage data associated with your account

Please note that some information may be retained for legal or administrative purposes, as required by law.
